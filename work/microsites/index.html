airG

The Airgames community and its suite of games were designed for the mobile. They are best experienced in the palm of your hands where your fingertips do all the talking. One of the consequences to our mobile first design approach was the lack of time and resources put towards the large screen experiences.

Our solution, was to create a new microsite for each product. These microsites would provide each product with an alternate starting experience, one better suited at helping us inform and acquire new users. Here are the links to view the live Airgames and Big Barn World pages.
