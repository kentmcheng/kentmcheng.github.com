airG

A chemistry themed clone of the original word game, Srcabble. This experimental web app was used to test the possibilities of gaming on the mobile web.

The bulk of the design work centered around the game's presentation. We wanted the game to not only look and feel like a native app, but to also behave like one too. Games are all about their interactions and engagement.

Here's one of my working prototypes. It let me explore, test, and demonstrate how the UI should transition and flow between its various screens and states.

The prototype relies heavily on the experimental WebKit tags and will only work on browsers that support it.
