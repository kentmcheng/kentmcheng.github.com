Canucks Next Game

canucks.kentc.me

In addition to updating the games schedule for the new season, I also refreshed the app's visuals. The new design takes inspiration from the official Canucks app.

October is a special month for us Canadians. Not only is it the month we celebrate Thanksgiving but it also marks the start of the new NHL season.

For my dad, each new season begins with the same old request to his son, to print him a copy of the Canucks game schedule. But now that he has a smartphone, I decided to make him a simple web app instead.

Just launch it and it automagically finds and scrolls to the next upcoming game in the list. This foolproof experience is perfect for my dad who isn't very adept with technology. It's nothing fancy, but he loves it and that's all that matters.
