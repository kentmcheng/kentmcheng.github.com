Big Barn World App

airG

Big Barn World isn't your typical farming game. Built for the web, it was one of the first online farming games for the mobile. It launched during a time when feature phones were still the norm and they had little to no apps. Now let's fast forward to the present—feature phones are dead, all phones are smart, and it's all about the apps.

The initial version of this app served more as a sidekick and companion to the game. The app provided access to key game info without needing to load the game. It served as the player's launchpad and starting point.

The app was designed for both Android and iOS, and can be downloaded on their respective app stores. The iOS version can be found here and the Android version now comes in two flavors—standard and classic.
