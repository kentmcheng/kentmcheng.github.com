Email Templates

airG

To match the Airgames brand and experience we decided to send visually rich HTML based emails. Email templates that we could reskin and reuse as needed.

As it turns out, the task to create these templates was a lot more challenging and time consuming than I ever expected. First, I had to learn about the limitations, which there were many. Next, was identifying the supported markup, which was extremely antiquated. And Lastly, was the actual coding and testing itself, which was just tedious. It is a known fact that these types of emails will break, so it was crucial to ensure the message always remained clear and readable.

Much thanks goes to MailChimp for their great email design reference and to Litmus for their great email previews.
