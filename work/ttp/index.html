Tag Team Poker

Tag Team Games

It's our take on a fresh new poker experience. Traditionally poker sites and games have always frowned upon collusion, so we wanted to encourage it. We also wanted to present the game in a similar fashion to televised poker, up close and personal. We called this our Under the Gun view.

The game was created in Unity, a cross-platform game engine and game creation system. The team consisted of just three members, a developer, designer, and artist. Being such a small team was very challenging, we had to wear many hats and fill many shoes. This is our first and hopefully not last game as indie developers.

Tag Team Poker is available on the Apple App Store and on the Google Play Store. It's free to play and will work on your phone or tablet, the bigger the screen the better!
