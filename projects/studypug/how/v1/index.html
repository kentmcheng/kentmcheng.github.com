Why learn using video tutorials?

Here at StudyPug, we understand learning from a textbook is tough. Most people are visual learners, so by combining visual examples with audio we create a better learning environment. Studies show that after 3 days, humans only retain 10-20% of information they read or hear, versus 50-65% when visuals are added.

Our teaching style

Our help videos are short and to the point. We focus on real, in-depth examples and problems — the kind you see on your exams, or from the back of your textbook — you know, the ones your teacher never seems to get to. The concepts aren’t hard, but the questions can be tricky — we show you how to solve them.
